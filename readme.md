# Sam Training Plan

This is your 3-Month "Belly Killer" Master Plan. At 179cm and 76kg, this plan will shift your body from "carrying a bit of a gut" to a lean 70kg with visible muscle definition.
1. The Weekly Schedule (5 Days On / 2 Days Off)
Stick to this rhythm to allow your 37-year-old joints to recover while keeping your metabolism high.

| Day | Workout Type | Intensity |
|---|---|---|
| Mon | REST | Recovery & 5k-8k Steps |
| Tue | Combo A | 20m Dumbbells + 10m Treadmill (12% Incline) |
| Wed | Swim | 30–40m Frog Style (Focus on long glides) |
| Thu | Combo B | 20m Dumbbells + 10m Treadmill (12% Incline) |
| Fri | REST | Recovery & 5k-8k Steps |
| Sat | Combo A | 20m Dumbbells + 10m Treadmill (12% Incline) |
| Sun | Swim | 30–40m Frog Style (Increase lap speed) |

------------------------------
2. The 20-Minute Dumbbell Circuit
Perform these 5 moves in a circle. Rest 60 seconds only after completing all five. Aim for 3–4 rounds.

   1. Goblet Squats (12 Reps): Hold 1 DB at chest height. Sit deep until thighs are parallel to the floor.
   <img src="images/global_squat.jpeg" width="60%">

   2. Dumbbell Lunges (10 Reps/Leg): Hold DBs at sides. Step forward until both knees hit 90 degrees.
   <img src="images/dumbbell-static-lunge.webp" width="100%">

   3. Overhead Press (10 Reps): Stand tall, push DBs from shoulders to ceiling. Squeeze glutes to protect your back.
   <img src="images/dumbbell-standing-overhead-press.webp" width="60%">

   4. Dumbbell Rows (12 Reps): Lean forward 45 degrees with flat back. Pull DBs to your hips, squeezing shoulder blades.
   <img src="images/bentoverrow_dumbbell_full_large.png" width="60%">

   5. Weighted Plank (45 Secs): Standard elbow plank with a weight plate or small DB on your lower back.
   <img src="images/plank.jpeg" width="60%">

------------------------------
3. The 10-Minute Treadmill "Belly Finisher"
Do this immediately after the weights while in a fasted state.

* Incline: Fixed at 12%.
* Speed: Start at 3.0 mph (Brisk walk).
* The Rule: No holding handrails. Swing arms to engage core.

------------------------------
4. Food Intake: The "70kg Goal" Diet
This is a High-Protein, Carb-Controlled plan.

* Fasted Morning: Workout on empty stomach (Water/Black Coffee only).
* Breakfast (Post-Workout): 200g Greek Yogurt + 250ml HL Milk + 2 Eggs + 15g Almonds + 2 Oat Bread + 3 Fruits.
* Lunch/Dinner: 1 Meat + 2 Veg + 0.5 Rice.
* Rest Day Tweak (Mon/Fri): Drop to 1 Oat Bread and Zero Rice for lunch/dinner.

------------------------------
5. Progression: How to Increase Difficulty
To hit 70kg, you must force your body to adapt every 4 weeks.

| Month | Dumbbell Weight | Treadmill Speed | Plank Difficulty |
|---|---|---|---|
| Month 1 | Start (e.g., 8kg-10kg) | 3.0 mph | Bodyweight or 2.5kg |
| Month 2 | Add 2kg to all lifts | 3.2 - 3.4 mph | Add 5kg plate |
| Month 3 | Add another 2kg | 3.5 mph / 1m Slow Run | Hold for 60 Secs |


* Month 1 Focus: Perfect form. Feel the "squeeze" in your muscles.
* Month 2 Focus: Strength. The weights should feel heavy by the 10th rep.
* Month 3 Focus: Intensity. This is the "stubborn fat" phase. Increase treadmill speed and minimize rest between sets.

Final Success Metrics

* Scale: Expect to lose 0.5kg per week (Total 6kg over 12 weeks).
* Mirror: Your shoulders will look wider and your stomach flatter because of the high protein and heavy rows/presses.
* Feel: You will notice you no longer get winded climbing stairs or swimming long distances.
